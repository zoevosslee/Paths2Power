Paths to Power

Mapping Activism: Black Women and Femmes

Paths to Power is a co-created platform that brings Brooklyn's Civil Rights legacy to life. By collaborating with stewards of spaces where Black women and femme activists led movements, we support archive preservation and invite the community to explore these histories through walking tours that celebrate resistance, community-building, and joy.
